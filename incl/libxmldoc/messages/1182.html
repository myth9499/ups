For avoid SOCKLEN_T error, we must to add the follow line in
"win32config.h":

        #define SOCKLEN_T size_t

For avoid "getcwd" warning add follow to same file:

        #include <direct.h>

For otherwise warning... close your eyes :-)

I attach a context-diff-file with "win32config.h" modifications.
