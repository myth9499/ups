  Yep, your DTD is wrong:

~/XML -> ./tester --valid Reverb.qm
qm.dtd:1: error: Content error in the external subset
<!DOCTYPE quasi_module [
^
qm.dtd:1: error: Extra content at the end of the document
<!DOCTYPE quasi_module [
^

 ....

The external subset (DTD) must follow production [30] of the XML
specification:
  http://www.w3.org/TR/REC-xml#sec-prolog-dtd

By removing the fist and last line of qm.dtd the input validated
without problem
