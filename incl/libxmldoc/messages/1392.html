Hello,

I've just installed libxml on IRIX 6.4 and have encountered a bit of a
problem.

I needed to use the native compiler and so it took me setting
GCC in my enviroment to NULL. That seems odd, I would think that an
option sent to the configure script would be better and more accesible.

It actually took me a while to figure this out, but once I did all
of the other problems went away. One was that all the test programs
had undefined references to zlib functions, because frankly gcc on
our system here has been poorly installed. Not to mention there
are three different and valid object file formats being used on this
system. I think alot of my problems were due to these things.

Anyway maybe all of this business with autoconf is intended, it actually
isn't that difficult once one knows what to do.
