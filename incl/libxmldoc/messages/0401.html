> I first reported this on the PHP4 mailing list, but after some more
> poking around, what I'm observing seems to be happening in the libxml
> library that PHP4 uses.
>
> Here's a sample document:
>
> <?xml version='1.0'?>
> <root>
> a<x> </x>b<x> </x>c
